Application Guide

Follow our simple steps to get the best results from your organic fertilizer

Step-by-Step Application

1

Soil Preparation

Moisten the soil slightly before application. Remove any weeds or debris from the area.

2

Application Rate

Apply 2-3 cups per 100 square feet for gardens, or 1 cup per plant for container gardens.

3

Mixing Method

Mix the fertilizer into the top 4-6 inches of soil using a garden fork or tiller.

4

Watering

Water thoroughly after application to help nutrients begin breaking down.

Best Practices

  • Apply in early morning or late evening
  • Avoid application before heavy rain
  • Reapply every 4-6 weeks during growing season

Application Schedule

Vegetables Every 4 weeks
Flowers Every 6 weeks
Trees & Shrubs Every 8 weeks
Lawn Every 6-8 weeks

Application Note

Always wear gloves and follow proper safety guidelines. Keep out of reach of children and pets. Store in a cool, dry place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our organic fertilizers

How often should I apply organic fertilizer?
Application frequency depends on the type of plants and soil conditions. Generally, we recommend applying our organic fertilizers every 4-6 weeks during the growing season. For specific guidelines, refer to the product instructions or consult our gardening guides.
Are your fertilizers safe for edible plants?
Yes, all our organic fertilizers are 100% safe for use on fruits, vegetables, and herbs. They contain no synthetic chemicals or harmful additives, making them ideal for organic food production.
How long does it take to see results?
While results vary, most users notice improved plant health within 2-3 weeks of application. Soil health improvements become more evident over time with regular use, typically within one growing season.
Can I use your fertilizers for indoor plants?
Yes, we offer specialized organic fertilizers suitable for indoor plants. These formulations are odor-free and specially balanced for container growing conditions.
How should I store your organic fertilizers?
Store our products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep containers tightly sealed when not in use. Most products have a shelf life of 2-3 years when stored properly.
